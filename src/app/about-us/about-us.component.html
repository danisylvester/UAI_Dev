UAI MIDDLE EAST, DMCC

UAI Middle East DMCC

was founded by Mr. S. K. Chauhan, an American entrepreneur, having his business in USA since 1998. He built a team of highly experienced executives to grow his international business in the field of Gold, Oil & Gas based in Dubai.

EXECUTIVE TEAM

Mr. S.K. Chauhan,
Chairman, CEO

He is a U.S. Citizen, born in India, and holds a Bachelor Degree in Electrical Engineering [BSEE] 1976-81 from Kurukshetra University, India. Also, he received Professional Engineer [PE] license from State of Tennessee, USA. Mr. Chauhan is a professional entrepreneur having experience working as a Marine Engineer in the Indian Merchant Navy from 1982 to 1985. He was a Consulting Engineer from 1985 to 1991, and a Chief Engineer from 1991 to 1998. He is the founder of UAI Inc. from 1998 to present in the USA and now the founder of UAI MIDDLE EAST in Dubai, U.A.E.



As UAI Inc. Mr. Chauhan designed and implemented Smart City Solutions to Utility Industry [Electric, Water and Gas], EMA, Police, Fire, Healthcare and Real Estate sectors in over 135 Cities in 23 States in USA, spanned across Alaska to Florida and Puerto Rico. He migrated to USA with 42 dollars in his pocket and achieved $83 Million USD in contracts as the sole proprietor of UAI, Inc. He sold his Utility Division to Trimble, current a market cap greater than 13 billion USD, [Stock Symbol NASDAQ: TRMB] for an undisclosed amount.



Now Mr. Chauhan is poised to grow his international business, having over 38 years of entrepreneurial experience, and travelled more than 25 countries, developing a vast international network in the area of Gold, Oil & Gas, and further into Healthcare IT, Smart City, commodity trading and project finance.
